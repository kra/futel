Church Of Robotron
2012 to 2015

The Church of Robotron is an ongoing collaborative series of immersive, interactive multimedia installations which present a cautionary message about machine superiority and human error. Effects include kinetic sculptures, whistles, percussion, vibration, ozone generators, electrical discharges, simulated fires, and projected video of the current and previous participants, and live performances, all triggered by audience interaction. Installations are customized for the space and opportunity, and can include unattended public interaction at all hours performed by the audience touching a plate glass window or manipulating a ruggedized control.

C.H.U.N.K. 666
1992 to 2015

C.H.U.N.K. 666 turns salvaged bicycles into working human powered vehicles and then destroys them in public gladiatorial contests. As cofounder and ongoing participant, I have led creative direction, fabricated custom vehicles, built sets, organized public events and festivals, created content, and led outreach and fundraising.
